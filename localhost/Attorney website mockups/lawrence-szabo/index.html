A LITTLE ABOUT US

The Law Office of Lawrence J. Szabo is a small, boutique law firm that emphasizes quality, personal and friendly legal services to clients needing assistance with business and real estate transactional and litigation related legal matters. Clients include individuals, sole proprietorships, partnerships, limited liability companies and corporations. The law firm provides responsive service and is dedicated to serving all clients with honesty and integrity.
