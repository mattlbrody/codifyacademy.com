A LITTLE ABOUT FAHEY LAW

The office of Fahey Law, PLLC, Darrell Fahey Attorney at Law works with families and individuals throughout Michigan. Darrell Fahey, has been practicing law since 2005. He can skillfully guide you toward a resolution of your legal concerns. Contact us to schedule a consultation. Darrell works out of two offices, one in Van Buren County and the other in Ionia County. He is actively practicing law in Van Buren, Kalamazoo, Ionia, Montcalm, Clinton, and Eaton Counties. Darrell has been an attorney since 2005 in Ionia, Montcalm, Clinton, and Eaton Counties. He received his Juris Doctorate Degree from Cooley Law School in Lansing and his Bachelor's Degree from Michigan State University in East Lansing. Darrell served in the United States Army after graduating from Carson City-Crystal High School. Darrell was born and raised in Ionia County, Michigan.
