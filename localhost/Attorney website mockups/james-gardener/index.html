A LITTLE ABOUT FAMILY SOLUTIONS LAW GROUP

Family Solutions was Founded in 2005 by James M. Gardener, Attorney at Law, MA, LMFT, as a vehicle for providing conections to positive and reliable family related services, products and information. Mr. Gardener has practiced law and worked with families in the Northern California area for over 21 years. Each family and each family issue is unique. We are hopeful that the resources discovered through Family Solutions and this web site provide helpful assistance for your particular family circumstance.

Services

Family Solutions Law Group provides full service family law representation by through and under the direction of James M. Gardener, JD, MA, MFT. Mr. Gardener is a licensed California attorney who has practiced law and has worked with families in the Northern California area for over 21 years. Mr. Gardener routinely practices in the Sacramento, Folsom, Rosevile, Rocklin, El Dorado Hills, El Dorado County, Davis, Dixon, Yolo County, Sutter County, Yuba County, Vacaville, Fairfield, Solano County, San Joaquin County, Stanislaus County, Sonoma County, Contra Costa County and Marin County areas.
